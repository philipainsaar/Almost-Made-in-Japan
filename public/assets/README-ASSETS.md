# AMIJ asset drop folder

Replace these placeholder files with your real Almost Made in Japan assets.

Default paths used by the app are configured in `lib/site-assets.ts`.

Suggested swaps:

- `amij-logo.svg` → your real round AMIJ logo
- `amij-mascot.svg` → your floating pink alien cat / shop mascot
- `hero-main.svg` → first large campaign image
- `hero-closeup.svg` → split/detail campaign image
- `your-photos.svg` → customer photos / social proof image
- `collection-dreamy.svg`, `collection-emo.svg`, `collection-nature.svg`, `collection-cyber.svg` → heart collection images
- Category images can come from Shopify collection images automatically. The local category SVGs are only fallbacks.
- Product images are loaded from Shopify products and collections.

You can use PNG, JPG, WEBP, SVG, or GIF assets. After changing filenames, update `lib/site-assets.ts`.
